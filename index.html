About Dr. Preeti Jain

“Preeti Jain is a global aviation and sustainability expert associated with IATA, where she focuses on advancing economic and policy frameworks for achieving net-zero carbon emissions in the aviation sector.”

Her work spans sustainable aviation fuel (SAF), climate policy, and global energy transition strategies, contributing to industry transformation through research, public policy engagement, and international collaboration.

She has been featured across leading global platforms including international media, policy forums, and industry bodies, and is recognized for her contributions to sustainable aviation, energy transition, and climate-focused economic strategies.
